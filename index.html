Revit is at the core of our production environment, where accuracy and interoperability come together.

Our Revit modeling services include:

  • Architectural, Structural, and MEP modeling up to LOD 400
  • Template creation and BIM execution standards (BEP)
  • Model optimization for performance and file efficiency
  • Sheet setup, schedules, and quantity take-offs
  • Coordination-ready models compatible with Navisworks and IFC exports

We help studios and contractors transform 2D information or conceptual designs into fully coordinated, data-rich BIM models, always aligned with ISO 19650 workflows.

For teams working in Graphisoft Archicad, we provide flexible modeling support tailored to the software's open and visual approach.

Our Archicad services cover:

  • Detailed architectural modeling using native Archicad tools
  • Model conversion and coordination using IFC or BCF workflows
  • Project setup and attribute management for consistent model data
  • Creation of custom GDL objects (parametric components)
  • With Archicad, we focus on maintaining clean, efficient models that enhance collaboration without compromising performance.

Vectorworks offers unique versatility, especially for design-oriented practices. Our Vectorworks BIM modeling services are designed to maximize precision while maintaining creative flexibility.

We provide:

  • BIM model setup and data structuring using Vectorworks Architect / Designer
  • Conversion of DWG, PDF, or SketchUp files into native BIM elements
  • Parametric object creation with Marionette
  • IFC model preparation and coordination with other BIM tools
  • Visual presentations, render setups, and data-tagging for documentation

We ensure your Vectorworks models are both visually compelling and technically robust, ready for coordination or IFC exchange.
